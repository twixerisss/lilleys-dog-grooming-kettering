About Us

Professional Dog Grooming

Lilley's Dog Grooming provides professional, caring grooming for dogs of all breeds in Kettering. City & Guilds Level 3 qualified and first aid trained, every pup gets 1-to-1 personalised attention.

From full grooms to puppy introductions, nail clipping to de-matting, we treat every dog as if they were our own.
