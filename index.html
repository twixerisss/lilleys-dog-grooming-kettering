Lilley’s is a one-dog-at-a-time grooming salon in Kettering, run by a City & Guilds Level 3 qualified and pet first-aid trained groomer. No crates of waiting dogs, no rush, no production line — just your pup getting full attention from start to finish.

Whether it’s a puppy’s first introduction, a teddy-bear trim on a Cockapoo, or careful de-matting on a coat that’s gone past the brush — every groom is shaped around the dog in front of us, not the other way around.

How it works.

First-time visitors get a quick chat about coat, behaviour and what kind of finish you’d like — nothing’s rushed, and there are no daft questions. Once we know your dog, rebooking is as simple as a text.
